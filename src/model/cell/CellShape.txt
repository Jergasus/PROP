CLASS: CellShape
Package: model.cell
Layer: Model

Purpose:
  Enum that identifies the geometry of the cells in a Board.
  Used by Cell.toString() to choose the correct display format
  and by Board to apply hex row-offset in toString().

Values:
  - SQUARE:   standard rectangular grid cell
  - HEXAGON:  offset hexagonal grid cell
  - TRIANGLE: triangular grid cell (orientation depends on (row+col) % 2)
