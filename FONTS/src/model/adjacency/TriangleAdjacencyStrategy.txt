CLASS: TriangleAdjacencyStrategy
Package: model.adjacency
Layer: Model

Purpose:
  Adjacency for triangular cells. Each cell has up to 3 neighbours: left, right,
  and one vertical neighbour whose direction depends on the cell's orientation.
  Cells where (row + col) % 2 == 0 point up and connect downward;
  cells where (row + col) % 2 == 1 point down and connect upward.
